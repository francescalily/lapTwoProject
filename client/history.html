Let's Explore Florin County

Redania District

Considered the capital of Florin County. Redania has a history of natural disasters and fending off invaders.

So what's the story with Redania?

Came to the wrong neighbourhood...

Florin's southern neighbours Alaska tried to invade through the highways that connected through Redania in the year 1990.

Flooding

Due to the attempted invasion, our bridges were blown up and ruined the Dams. This caused the entire region to experience high levels of flooding and over 200 Redanians had lost their lives as a result of this war...

Causation

But due to the floods, we now have enveloped springs at centre of Florin! These springs have boosted our exports largely increasing the GDP by 15%. The urban areas of Cintra wouldnt exist if the war had never taken place.
